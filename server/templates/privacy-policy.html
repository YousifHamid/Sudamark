Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2026

Introduction

Sudamark respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

1. Account Information

If you choose to create an account, we collect:

  • Mobile phone number

This information is required to create and secure your account and enable login functionality.

We do not collect or store your password in plain text. Passwords are securely processed and protected using appropriate security measures.

2. Guest Access

You may use certain features of the app without creating an account. When using the app as a guest, we do not collect personal account information such as phone number.

3. Device and Usage Information

We may automatically collect limited technical information necessary to operate and improve the app, such as:

  • Device type
  • Operating system version
  • Basic app usage data
  • Error and crash logs

This information is used only to maintain app performance, security, and reliability.

How We Use Information

  • • Create and manage user accounts
  • • Authenticate login credentials
  • • Provide and maintain app functionality
  • • Improve performance and fix technical issues
  • • Protect against unauthorized access and fraud

Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except when required by law or to protect the security and integrity of the application.

Data Retention

We retain account information as long as your account remains active.

Users may request to:

  • Delete their account
  • Update their account information
  • Suspend their account

Upon verified request, account data will be deleted or updated within a reasonable timeframe unless retention is required by law.

Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, misuse, alteration, or disclosure.

All data is transmitted using secure encryption (HTTPS).

Your Rights

  • Request deletion of your account
  • Request updates to your personal information
  • Stop using the app at any time

To submit any data-related request, please contact us at the email below.

Children’s Privacy

Sudamark is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
